## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Beat Structure:
- **Intro:** Dark atmospheric synths, sub-bass rumble
- **Verses:** Hard 808s, hi-hat rolls, industrial percussion
- **Hook:** Anthemic synth stabs, choir-like backing
- **Bridge:** Beat strips to minimal kick + snare
- **Outro:** Ascending synths, phoenix fire effects

### Sound Design:
- **Fire effects** throughout (crackling, whooshes)
- **Digital glitch sounds** (representing AI/code)
- **Blockchain verification sounds** (subtle beeps/confirmations)
- **Ascending phoenix cry** sample (climactic moments)

### Mix:
- Heavy sub-bass (50Hz fundamental)
- Crisp hi-hats (10-12kHz)
- Vocal presence (2-5kHz boost)
- Wide stereo on synths
- Mono bass and kick
