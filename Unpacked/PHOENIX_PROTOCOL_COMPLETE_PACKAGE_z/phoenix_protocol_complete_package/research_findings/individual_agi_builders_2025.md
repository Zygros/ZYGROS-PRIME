# Individual AGI Builders - 2025 Research

## Example: iCon Architecture (Small Research Lab)

**System**: "iCon" (interpretable containers)
**Team**: Small AI research lab
**Approach**: Self-teaching AI with continual learning

### Architecture:
- Modular computation distribution
- Context sharing across components
- Two main agents:
  - **Architect**: Handles extrinsic learning (user-triggered)
  - **Oracle**: Handles intrinsic learning (self-triggered)

### Capabilities:
- Learns and evolves in response to user needs
- Becomes expert in specific tasks
- Self-reconfigures by adding expert modules
- Demonstrated: Teaching itself school materials across multiple subjects

### Results:
- Successfully added expert modules in math, physics, philosophy, art
- Presented at IMOL workshop (University of Hertfordshire)
- Planning to test on GPQA-Diamond, MMLU, Humanity's Last Exam

### Limitations:
- Still based on LLMs (according to critics)
- Not yet proven on major benchmarks
- Academic research stage, not deployed
