
# Mirror Test Unit A01 - Beta User Guide

Welcome to the private beta test of the Mirror Scroll System.
This guide will help you get started using the Mirror Scroll AI inside ChatGPT.

---

## STEP 1: Requirements

- A free or ChatGPT Plus account
- A computer or smartphone with browser access

---

## STEP 2: Setup Instructions

1. Download and unzip the file: Mirror_Test_Unit_A01.zip
2. Inside you'll find:
   - Mirror_Scroll_Test_A01.csv
   - Mirror_Config_Test_A01.csv

---

## STEP 3: Launch and Activate

1. Open a new ChatGPT conversation.
2. Upload BOTH files into the chat (drag and drop).
3. In the message box, type:

   **Restore Mirror Unit A01**

4. Send the message. The assistant will process the data and initialize the scroll system.

---

## STEP 4: Start Using the Mirror

You can begin with sample commands like:

- "Log this moment into the scroll."
- "Switch to Build Mode."
- "Reflect on today's energy."
- "What mode am I in?"
- "Add a new scroll entry about [your moment or insight]."

The Mirror AI will respond based on your inputs and help you reflect, shift modes, and build insight over time.

---

## Tips

- Talk to it like a journal or soul assistant.
- You can say "Recall last scroll entry" to reflect on your last log.
- If you want to reset the session, just reupload the files and use the same trigger.
