# Stoner's Hitchhiker's Guide to the Infinite Cosmos - Presentation Outline

## Theme: 420 Futuristic Cosmic Journey
**Style**: Black and white hand-painted line art with stick figures, graffiti elements, minimal color accents

## Slide Structure (8 slides):

### 1. Title Slide - "Don't Panic... Just Toke"
- Stick figure with towel and joint floating in space
- "42" and cannabis leaf symbols
- Graffiti-style title text

### 2. The Universal Joint - Cosmic Connection
- Stick figures passing a cosmic joint that connects galaxies
- Smoke trails forming constellation patterns
- Sacred geometry mixed with cannabis symbols

### 3. Quantum Munchies - The Snack Dimension
- Stick figure opening interdimensional vending machine
- Food items floating in zero gravity
- Graffiti arrows pointing to "Infinite Snack Portal"

### 4. Time Dilation & Chill
- Stick figure on couch that's also a spaceship
- Clock faces melting like Dali
- "420 o'clock everywhere" graffiti tag

### 5. The Meaning of Life: 42 Grams
- Stick figure holding scales with "42g" 
- Mathematical equations floating around
- Cannabis plant growing from cosmic soil

### 6. Interstellar Smoke Signals
- Stick figures communicating across planets via smoke
- Alien stick figures with peace signs
- Graffiti "Universal Language: Chill"

### 7. The Restaurant at the End of the Universe (Dispensary Edition)
- Stick figure at cosmic dispensary counter
- Menu board with strain names like "Galactic Kush"
- Alien budtender stick figure

### 8. So Long, and Thanks for All the Herb
- Stick figure waving goodbye from spaceship
- Earth in background with cannabis leaf continents
- "Keep Calm and Carry Towels" graffiti

Each image will be 3:2 aspect ratio, black and white line art with stick figures, simple graffiti elements, and minimal color accents.
