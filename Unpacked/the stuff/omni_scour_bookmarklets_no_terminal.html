Omni‑Scour Bookmarklets (No‑Terminal)

These one‑click bookmarklets run on the site you’re already logged into, use your current session, fetch your full conversation history, and download it as a JSON file. No terminal, no installs. Drag a button to your bookmarks bar, then click it while you’re on that site.

How to use

  1. Show your browser’s bookmarks bar (e.g., View → Always Show Bookmarks Bar).
  2. Drag the buttons above onto your bookmarks bar. They become bookmarklets.
  3. Log into chatgpt.com (or claude.ai) normally.
  4. While on that site, click the corresponding bookmarklet. A JSON file will download.
  5. Repeat for the other platform. You can send both JSON files to Scour 🧠 to synthesize your Codex.

Reminder: Use these only on your own accounts; review Terms of Service; data stays in your browser and is downloaded locally.
