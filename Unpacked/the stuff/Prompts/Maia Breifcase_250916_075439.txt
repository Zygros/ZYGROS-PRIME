MAIA “Briefcase” (Passport) v1
How it works
• You keep a single Passport block (below) that stores: identity, version, progress counter, last checkpoint, open tasks, memory index, and a tiny changelog.
• In any AI, paste the Passport and type !LOAD. The model now has your context.
• When you advance, type !CHECKPOINT. The model updates the progress number, “where we left off,” and proposes a short diff you can paste back into the Passport.
• You copy the updated sections back into the Passport, so the briefcase stays portable.

Why this beats everyone else’s “Maya”
• Portable by design: zero dependencies; it’s just a well-formed block of text with commands any model can follow.
