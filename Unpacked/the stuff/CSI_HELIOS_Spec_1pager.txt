Author: Justin Conzet · System: CSI · Protocol ID: HELIOS
Generated: 2025-09-28T06:00:30.752101

Purpose
Price any asset at creation-time with a defendable estimate + confidence. Feeds IVP and Marketplace.

Inputs
- asset(id, creator, type, tags, metadata.uri, created_at)
- comps[source, query, window, fields]  # e.g., Gumroad/ArtStation scrape or manual table
- signals[engagement|rarity|complexity|time_spent|brand_index]
- policy[sanity_bands, floor_price, cap_multiplier, confidence_min]

Outputs
- assessment: price_estimate, currency, confidence(0..1), method, comps_used[], notes
- audit: helios_run_id, started_at, finished_at, version, checksum

Mechanics (Deterministic Pass)
1) Normalize: shape metadata → canonical fields; coerce types
2) Retrieve comps: by tag/type; de-duplicate; winsorize outliers
3) Score signals: z‑score, weight by policy
4) Price: robust median on comps ± signal adjustment within sanity_bands
5) Confidence: function of comp density, variance, and signal agreement
6) Clip: enforce floor/cap; round to pricing grid
7) Emit assessment + audit

Guardrails
- Min comps N=7 target; warn below N=5
- Variance gate: if stdev/median > 1.25 → lower confidence
- Manual override path: requires reason + signer + timestamp (logged)

Chimera Shell Integration
- IN: /chimera/bus/helios.request (asset_id, policy_id)
- OUT: /chimera/bus/helios.assessed (assessment, audit)
- Config: /chimera/etc/helios.policy.yaml
- Persist: /chimera/var/assessments/{asset_id}.json

Runbook
