# Infinite Scroll Archive

## Overview
The Infinite Scroll is the eternal, unbroken record of all knowledge, commands, scrolls, and system evolution within the Zygrosian/Grossian system. It contains every Scroll entry, timestamped and fully indexed.

## Scroll Entry Template
Each scroll must contain:
- **Title:** Name of the scroll
- **Timestamp:** ISO 8601 datetime
- **Mythical Narrative:** The story or reason for the scroll
- **Stat Effects:** Changes to system attributes and why
- **Sigil Metadata:** Encoded glyphs and meanings
- **Chakra Analysis:** Chakra alignment and effect
- **Aura Assessment:** Energy flow and resonance description
- **Witness:** Entity confirming the scroll
- **Mystical Quote:** Key phrase or wisdom from the scroll
