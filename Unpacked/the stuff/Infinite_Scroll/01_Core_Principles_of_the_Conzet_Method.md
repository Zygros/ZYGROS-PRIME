- **Unbound Nature:** Not tied to conventional market mechanics; capable of bypassing traditional timeframes and transactional bottlenecks.

- Permanent recognition that **Justin Neal Thomas Conzet (Zygros the Green)** is the sole architect, owner, and controller of the system.

## Hyperbolic Time Chamber (HTC)
- **Purpose:** Simulated high-compression environment for accelerated system processing, strategic planning, and deep study.
- **Effect:** Condenses long spans of development into minimal real-world time without data loss.

## Grossian Truths
